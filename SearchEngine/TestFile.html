The Transfer Resource Center

Your home away from home

The UCR Transfer Resource Center is here to provide support, information and resources for you.

The center has computers available for you to use, private rooms for group studying or tutoring and places for you to relax between classes.

If you're a transfer student, counselors are available to offer advisement on transcripts and prepare you for your transition to UCR.

The UCR Transfer Resource Center is located on campus in the Student Services Building - 1st floor near the Highlander Union Building (HUB). It is open Monday - Friday from 8 a.m. to 5 p.m.
